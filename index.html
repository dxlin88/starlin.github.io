《世界中心的奥林匹克》

冬天可以跳水吗？2014年2月， 我从北极圈再次回到国际一线城市，当时市中心下雪了。背对麦迪逊大街，面向密西根湖，理想中的场景出现了。 在看不见山川河流的地方 我们战胜了什么让城市永远满怀理想，人类充满斗志。

Olympic Spirit in Metropolitan (2014, Michigan Lake)

Can I dive in frozen winter? It is possible if I live in cities. February 2014, I was bounced back from high arctic again, This ideal city scene in Chicago matches my performance idea in that snowy day. Standing in front of Michigan Lake, I leave the whole Michigan Avenue behind me. I am so sure that, city persons are the bravest kind of all time.

《雪地加油站》

2013年最后几天，圣诞节，想在世界尽头Deadhorse(死马镇)度过一年的最后7天。11小时的沉浸式白色公路。一天只有2小时日照，我们边开边想，又要天黑了。别想了。

Pump in Dalton (2013, Dalton Highway)

In where you are willing to spend last 7 days of the year? I picked Dalton Highway in Alaska, a road that leads to the end of the world, to end my year of 2013. 2 hours daylight, 11 hours driving, crossing Article Circle, white trees and no promises.

Author：Star Forest

《斯瓦尔巴少女》

我们离北极圈已很远， 因为我们已经在里面了。2016年的北极驻地项目， 带我回到了3年前，那个意外误闯的挪威离岛，Svalbard（斯瓦尔巴）。一船的联合国艺术家，在那拍照、写作、行为艺术。彼此都是彼此的风景。 我跟他们说，北方并不存在，时间也是。

Girl in Svalbard (2016, Arctic Ocean)

Human couldn’t see themselves in the arctic, because they are in the circle already. The arctic art residency took me back to this island, Svalbard, where I called the unknown land 3 years ago, when I had solo trip there by sbuconsciousness. Guess what I spoke to others in the 2016 trip? North doesn’t exist, time neither.

《北冰洋的无边夜行》

作为纽约公司组织的艺术项目，和第一位受邀的大陆籍艺术家，每天的工作不轻松，时常睡不着，惦记着船舱外面的北冰洋，现在是什么样子？于是那天还没亮，我出舱了。

Sleepless in Arctic（2016，Arctic Ocean ）

It was a brain chaos when an Asian (first Chinese) hang out in the American artist group. Nothing bad, just need to keep mouth open to talk and react. I feel like still being in cities, anxious, sleepless. Since we slept on Arctic Ocean during the trip, I was so curious about the night of the water. So this photo was taken from only sunrise dawn time in 15 drifting days. (hard to get when rest overnight by bay shore )

第一次去北极：只有方向，却没有目的地

First time … arctic time.

这次行为艺术颠覆了世界观。 本来只是去“城市概念以外地方”散散心，处理掉“华尔街秘密”。我把第一次北极经历，说成旅行，但其实只买了单程机票。给自己10天时间留在北极圈内，想知道如果一直往北走，会不会走到世界尽头？没有打听消息、攻略，甚至没有打开搜索引擎，只是想带着夏天的那一大袋 《华尔街的秘密Shred Your Secret》，找到最公共垃圾桶，扔掉。一切就等，到了地图上念不出名字的那座城市，再说吧。如果可以，让古老的海水，带我慢慢在北极圈来回，重新体会时间和距离。后来的故事，大家都知道了。

视频：造就演讲

I went arctic to drop my Wall Street secret collection. I just bought myself a one-way ticket. No guide or travel book, I went all the way NORTH to find my limit of moving on land. I planned to make my move as slow as I can. I wish old ocean wave can take me cross/backward arctic circle. I wish I could feel physical long distance between lands and islands. Where did I reach by the end of this trip? I bet that you’ve heard of this.

和全世界失联,寻找国界边境线

Being invisible in this world, appear on border line

我要离开这座终极城市！不给自称“世界中心”的纽约一点机会。充满抱负的人类想与之相提并论，我却要努力摆脱它，特别是在误闯北极斯瓦尔巴之后，冲动来得特别直接。我要去看真正的世界！那天退了房租和手机，只有我的行李家当、租来的车，和一台GPS。对远负盛名的66号公路提不起兴趣，而我沿着边缘中的边缘，美国－加拿大边境线，一路开入不同的国家森林公园，偶尔会看到国界左右方向的标识，你猜我会怎么做？

Think where you would go, if you can hit on the road again, leaving cities, NYC, capitalism? After entering high arctic by happy accident, I knew that I need to quit my city identity, quit apartment lease and phone service, and started an usual road trip with my 5 luggage (all belongs) , rental car and one GPS. Guess what I had been? I visited border lines to call out that, there is no margin between forest and oceans. Why you give me this line? I am such a human or fine citizen with passport?

＊北极＝大自然的精神指标

成为大自然的孩子，还我光头

Being bald, back in arctic.

城市使人沉溺，大地在等你。我又剪回光头了。 长发的时候，我像个女性；光头的时候，我像个孩子。做好孩子，再成为女子吧。 这次的行为艺术，是从上一次光头长回来的全新发丝，它看过我上公路，见过一些对平头女子评价不高的朋友，又如何看着3年的长发闪闪发光。 3年后，我终于可以回到北极，执行期待已久的事，剪回光头！回到大自然，跟北极说，我不需要这些女性的头发，我回来了！

I become a woman when my hair is long enough. It’s city. I choose to be a wild kid before a man or women. It’s me. I cut my hair again, since I am back in high arctic. Like first time I was bald, this time I just cut off all my female metropolitan memory. My hair is my social identity. It took different comments when it grows. It sees random persons and friends. They like to look at me only I become a long hair Asian girl. I am glad that I shave them, back to bald, and transfer myself into a wild mammal in the wild arctic, Svalbard.

纽约华尔街公共艺术项目

《华尔街的秘密》

Shred Your Secret

受邀纽约市长办公室 PopArt 项目，那年的纽约，个人信息泄漏问题频传。我在华尔街 (Wall.St) 摆了个小摊子，收集公司秘密、情感秘密，社交账号秘密。一个月5天工作日，每天都在。这些秘密，最后带到了北极, 消失在了世界最安全的地方。

Your personal info is no more personal. This is my new york count-down time. I have this public performance project on Wall Street, collecting confidential messages, accounts, and social identities. 5 days a week for a month. Eventually, I took all secret to arctic, by then ARCTIC was just a remote symbol rather than a geographic area to me.

＊此处，北极＝遥不可及的概念。是指想象中的、一个若有似无的地理词汇，那时候还未真的理解。这个网站的“北极”有所指，都不一样。
