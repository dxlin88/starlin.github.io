OneMen

《世界中心的奥林匹克》

冬天可以跳水吗？2014年2月， 我从北极圈再次回到国际一线城市，当时市中心下雪了。背对麦迪逊大街，面向密西根湖，理想中的场景出现了。 在看不见山川河流的地方 我们战胜了什么让城市永远满怀理想，人类充满斗志。

Olympic Spirit in Metropolitan (2014, Michigan Lake)

Can I dive in frozen winter? It is possible if I live in cities. February 2014, I was bounced back from high arctic again, This ideal city scene in Chicago matches my performance idea in that snowy day. Standing in front of Michigan Lake, I leave the whole Michigan Avenue behind me. I am so sure that, city persons are the bravest kind of all time.

AlasgaGas

《雪地加油站》

2013年最后几天，圣诞节，想在世界尽头Deadhorse(死马镇)度过一年的最后7天。11小时的沉浸式白色公路。一天只有2小时日照，我们边开边想，又要天黑了。别想了。

Pump in Dalton (2013, Dalton Highway)

In where you are willing to spend last 7 days of the year? I picked Dalton Highway in Alaska, a road that leads to the end of the world, to end my year of 2013. 2 hours daylight, 11 hours driving, crossing Article Circle, white trees and no promises.

IceNight

《斯瓦尔巴少女》

我们离北极圈很远了， 因为我们已经在里面了。2016年的北极驻地项目， 带我回到了3年前，那个意外误闯的挪威离岛，Svalbard（斯瓦尔巴）。一船的联合国艺术家，在那拍照、写作、行为艺术。彼此都是彼此的风景。 我跟他们说，北方并不存在，时间也是。

Girl in Svalbard (2016, Arctic Ocean)

Human couldn’t see themselves in the arctic, because they are in the circle already. The arctic art residency took me back to this island, Svalbard, where I called the unknown land 3 years ago, when I had solo trip there by sbuconsciousness. Guess what I spoke to others in the 2016 trip? North doesn’t exist, time neither.

MagicWave

《北冰洋的无边夜行》

作为纽约公司组织的艺术项目，和第一位受邀的大陆籍艺术家，每天的工作不轻松，时常睡不着，惦记着船舱外面的北冰洋，现在是什么样子？于是那天还没亮，我出舱了。

Sleepless in Arctic（2016，Arctic Ocean ）

It was a brain chaos when an Asian (first Chinese) hang out in the American artist group. Nothing bad, just need to keep mouth open to talk and react. I feel like still being in cities, anxious, sleepless. Since we slept on Arctic Ocean during the trip, I was so curious about the night of the water. So this photo was taken from only sunrise dawn time in 15 drifting days. (hard to get when rest overnight by bay shore )
